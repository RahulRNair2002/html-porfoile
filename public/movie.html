The Best Movies According to Rahul

My Top 3 movies of all-time

EKO

“Eeko (2025)” is a tense survival drama where a man must confront his past and his instincts while fighting
against nature and human threats in a remote, unforgiving setting

F1

“F1” (2025) follows a former Formula One driver who returns to the sport to mentor a young talent while
chasing one last shot at glory on the world’s fastest racing stage.

Dhurandar

“Dhurandar” is a high-octane action thriller about a fearless man who takes on a powerful nexus of crime and
corruption using intelligence, brutality, and strategy.
